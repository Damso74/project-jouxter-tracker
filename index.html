Description:

This scenario outlines the project completion within 36 weeks. The Gantt chart highlights:

  • Key Milestones: Clear timelines for back-office and front-office tasks.
  • Critical Path: Highlighted in red, showing dependencies that could impact the project timeline.
  • Additional Delays: Illustrated with striped yellow sections to indicate extra time due to scope changes.
